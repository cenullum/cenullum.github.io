Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This policy explains how data is collected, stored, and used in My Photo Nonogram Puzzle.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled in the game My Photo Nonogram Puzzle.

Scope

This policy applies only to the My Photo Nonogram Puzzle game.

Information We Collect

To provide the game's functionality, we may store:

  • User-selected nicknames.
  • Puzzle images created, uploaded, or generated by users.

We do not require users to provide real names or other directly identifying personal information.

Data Storage

User-generated images and nicknames may be stored using Firebase services to provide game functionality and preserve user content.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their stored content. Associated nicknames and puzzle images will be removed from active systems within a reasonable period.

The application may provide an in-app option allowing users to delete their stored content directly.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase: Used for storage and application functionality. Firebase may process data according to Google's policies.
  • AdMob by Google: Used to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Opt-In

By installing and using My Photo Nonogram Puzzle, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Opt-Out

You may stop all future collection of information by uninstalling the application and deleting your stored content where applicable.
